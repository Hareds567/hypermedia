Marvel Cinematic
Universe

SUNY Oswego hypermedia design class’s members are building
a website to function as a key information source for the
users regarding marvel movies. The series features standalone movies
for the various superheroes and crossovers where the different characters
of the movies will be illustrated in the gallery of the website.

This project will let viewers experience a virtual reality
where they can see the worldbuilding and the history of
the MCU through the lens of Google Maps. Whether it’s visiting a
specific location of one of the films or using smartphones to see the
site of specific events. Through the use of AR the Marvel Cinematic
Universe can crossover into the real world.
